Hatchapuri

Description

This incredible pie is an intermediate Georgian dish. You'll make it every week!

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 500g Imereti cheese (or ricotta as a substitute)
  • 3 eggs
  • 100 g Cheddar cheese
  • 200 ml milk
  • Knob of butter

Steps

  1. Crack an egg into the flour and add milk slowly to make a dough ball.
  2. Leave the dough to stand in a warm place for 30 mins.
  3. Form into a boat shape and crack an egg in the middle.
  4. Sprinkle cheese on top and put into a pre-heated oven at Gas Mark 6 for 40 minutes until golden brown.
