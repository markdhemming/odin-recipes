Shepherd's Pie

Description

This incredible pie is an intermediate British dish. Plus it feeds the whole family!

Ingredients

  • 200g potatoes
  • 500g lamb mince
  • Handful of frozen mixed vegetables
  • 100 g cheese
  • Lamb stock cube
  • 50 ml milk
  • Knob of butter
  • 50 ml red wine (optional)

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and fry until brown.
  2. Peel and chop the potatoes and add to a pan of water. Bring to the boil, then cook for 20 minutes or until soft, then drain.
  3. Add the mince to the onions, cook until brown, and then mix in the stock cube and the frozen vegetables. Add the wine.
  4. Mash the potatoes and stir in the butter and milk.
  5. Put the meat mixture in a deep baking dish and spoon the mashed potato on top.
  6. Grate the cheese on top and place in the oven at Gas Mark 6 for 25 minutes.
