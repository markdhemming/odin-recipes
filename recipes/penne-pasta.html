Penne Pasta

Description

This easy-to-make classic is a starting point for anyone learning to cook. Plus it's extremely tasty!

Ingredients

  • 500g dry pasta
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 100 g cheese
  • Mixed herbs to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the onions into small cubes and fry until brown.
  2. Boil half a pan of water and add the pasta when the water starts to simmer. Cook for 6 minutes, then drain.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to the onions, stir for one minute and then mix into the drained pasta.
  4. Serve in bowls and grate the cheese on top.
